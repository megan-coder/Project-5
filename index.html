6 Word Memoir: Soda! It makes you throw up.

Soda is an unhealthy drink although it’s delicious it makes you throw up if you have too much and I know that from experience. I recall a time when my cousin and I were bored out of our minds at our grandparent’s picnic. We were forced to go with them ( I only went because my mom was gonna pick me up from there). The picnic was mainly centered around the adults, so we had nothing to do, and when I say nothing I mean NOTHING. We were both about to die of boredom until we decided to do a competition.
It started out as we were hungry so we were going to eat something then it became a competition. She had challenged me to a chug off! I accepted knowing I would probably lose because my stomach was smaller at the time, but I took that chilled, mouth-watering Fanta and chugged my way to victory. After my cousin asked for a rematch so I agreed to do a few more rounds. We continued to drink different types of sodas each round. Then we were tied and I thought I was about to die, but at least I would die without an empty stomach.
In the end, we both ended up throwing up and having to use the bathroom so much that our grandma made us wear diapers. We also ran almost 80 million laps from all the sugar. But even now my cousin and I continue to debate on who won.

Long Ago The 8 Planets Lived in Harmony Until The Sun People Attacked. They might just let me in. “What's The Password” I hear a gruff voice say What password I thought to myself. “Down with the Sun Nation?” I ask “Welcome to training” “What do mean training” I ask “ Well it’s combat training of course” “Ohhh that makes sense” “Meet your leaders,” Said the man From what I am told these are my leaders Luna can cast darkness over any area. Tomorrow is when we attack. I try to get a good night’s sleep but I can’t get over the fact that about 5 days ago I thought that this war would never end but now I’m about to end. Today is the day. Quickly and Quietly we plan our strategy. Trish and her team (that includes me) Will take out the guards. Lexi and her team will then go for the sun lord. “Remember to trust yourselves and don’t be afraid.” Said Trish Our plan worked! “We Won!” Now the war has ended And I learned something during all this. I learned to never doubt myself and to always stand up for what is right.
